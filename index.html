WEBSITE OF 2 UP ARTY BTY NCC
2 UP Arty Bty NCC has a glorious past being one of the oldest NCC Units of Allahabad. The Unit came into existence in 1952 and has many achievements to its credit. It has been inspected by numerous dignitaries, including late Shri Jawaharlal Nehru. Dr Harivansh Rai Bachchan has also served as ANO of this unit.The Unit is designed to provide specialised training in the handling and operation of artillery guns, and enrols undergraduate students up to the maximum strength of 200. The cadets are also attached to Army units for training. A large number of cadets have been regularly participating in Republic Day parades and contingents and winning prizes.

 The Unit is commanded by Lt Col. Chetan Dewan and provides NCC training exclusively to the students of the University, under the supervision of faculty members appointed as Associate NCC Officers, and with the help of serving defence personnel.  The Units are located near  NCC Group Headquarters complex in the Chatham Lines Campus of the University. The Associate NCC Officer from the faculty is Lt. (Dr) Raju Parghi  (Department of English).
